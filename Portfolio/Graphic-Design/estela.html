About the project

Estela Appointment Booking is an application designed for healthcare professionals, serving as a digital agenda where they can schedule appointments with their patients and send notifications. In 2020, I joined the Estela team as an art director, responsible for developing the entire visual communication of the brand on social media, using motion graphics and other creative materials that reflect the brand's identity.

During my tenure as an art director at Estela Appointment Booking, I was able to bring the brand's visual identity to life on social media. Through motion graphics and other fun visual elements, we effectively communicated the benefits and features of the platform while maintaining an approach that reflected the brand's personality and purpose. My work helped strengthen Estela's online presence and create a closer connection with healthcare professionals and their patients.
