About the project

Serzzare Semijoias, a prominent e-commerce platform specializing in high-quality semi-precious jewelry, caters to a discerning clientele nationwide. In 2017, I assumed the role of graphic designer at Serzzare, focusing primarily on building a sophisticated brand identity for the company on social media platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, and WhatsApp. My efforts were directed towards emphasizing the inherent luxury and sophistication of the brand through visually captivating content.

Throughout my tenure, I dedicated myself to infusing Serzzare's social media presence with an aura of sophistication and luxury. By meticulously curating and designing content that resonated with the brand's ethos, I succeeded in portraying Serzzare Semijoias as a symbol of refined elegance and opulence in the digital realm. This concerted effort not only bolstered the brand's online visibility but also enhanced its appeal among discerning consumers seeking luxurious semi-precious jewelry.
